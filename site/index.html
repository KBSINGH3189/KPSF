Located in the city of Ranchi in Jharkhand, Prime Trust focuses on programs that empower local women, enable rural and urban poor to become self-reliant, raise social awareness, and educate underprivileged children. Each year our reach grows, and more people are helped by its services due to collaboration with volunteers and other grassroots NGOs, as well as putting the power of change in the hands of the people who need it.
