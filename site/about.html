About us

Kulprakash Singh Foundation (KPSF) is setup in memory of our beloved Kulprakash Singh. He was a meritorious student, an exceptional Engineer and a true gentleman. In his memory, we, the family members have started this memorial foundation to provide free education and health services to underprivileged children and youth of our society. The major focus of KPSF is on children and youth, however it is open to all people who desire to become literate and aware.

KPSF strives to make a significant contribution in the area of education, women empowerment, health safety and Environment. The initiatives we take to achieve the foundation's goal are free education, free medical check up camps, blood donation camps, skill development classes and tree plantation campaigns.

The organization currently work around Ranchi district of Jharkhand only. It is well known that poverty and illiteracy rate in Jharkhand is high. Major population is not well aware of various government welfare schemes. KPSF works to create awareness about such welfare schemes and provide support in its effective utilization.
