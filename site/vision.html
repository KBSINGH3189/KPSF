Vision and Objective

KPSF is established with a vision of providing educated, healthy and safe environment to underprivileged children and youth of society. Women empowerment and upliftment of poor are major objectives of this organization. We strive to provide all necessary means to poor children and youth who are enthusiastic in chasing their dreams. Our mission is to educate the less privileged children and youth so that help in eradicating their poverty and contribute in nation’s growth.

Jharkhand is gripped with two major problems today, poverty and illiteracy. There are many areas where poverty rate is high and literacy rate is low. Our vision is to bridge this gap.

As we know, women empowerment is very necessary for the overall development of the society as women are the key members of a family and a society as well.

The empowerment of women starts on the benches of school with quality education and access to culture and information. Under this organization, women from the underprivileged society will get free school education along with skill development classes and a safe living.

Our aim is to make the poor children and youth capable enough to stand at their own feet in a decent way. We aim to make them capable enough so that they will no longer be marked as “underprivileged”.
